About Me

Hi, my name is Justin Maines. I'm a 14 year old student from Pennsylvania.
Whenever I was 5 years old, I discovered the Internet. I got this new toy, a Tech Deck, and my mom found a video on YouTube of people playing with Tech Decks. Starting then, I became infatuated with YouTube. When I was 6 or 7, I got my first laptop, and that made my interest in technology even higher. When I was 10, my dad told me about a website called CodeCademy. I completed a few of the web development courses, but I was too young to fully understand the content. However, this lead to me getting interested in programming. I started trying different things, I even made a simple 2d game wiht Unity. Shortly after, I quit trying to learn code, because I was angry at how little process I would make, and how little of the information I would understand. I retained basic HTML princibles, but couldn't do much aside from that. 2 or 3 years later, I discovered freeCodeCamp. I started the course around 6 months ago, and that is where I have learned 90% of what I know. Now that I've gotten a bit older, I can better understand the content and have the ability to retain what I'm learning. As of now, I've almost completed the Front End Development certification, and I'm eager to learn more! Thanks for stopping by my website.
